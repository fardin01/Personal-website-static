How I started coding!

The first thing I came into touch with was HTML. I could write reasonably good HTML within a few days and that was the all idea of coding I had. I didn't even know the names of different programming languages and I thought that was ALMOST all it. Few turn of events and I got to know ruby and ruby on rails. Very very cluttered and blury view of the whole thing in the first week. but it needed time to sink in.

Now that I look back a year ago, I can reflect of my performance. Now I realize that a little focus and dedication would go a long way. In order to solve a problem one first needs to understand the problem. A year ago it was very difficult for me to understand the problem, and through frustration I thought that I was not competent enough to write code make a computer do what I want! But today I am absolutely certain that I can do it, coding is what I want to do, and I'll be good at it.

A journey of a thousand miles begins with one step, and that is always and always the most difficult step. I am trying to take that first step now by entering the tech industry as a developer. I had this great and amazing chance a year ago and I would like to believe that I did all I could, but sometimes it is very difficult to judge whether you have done your best or not.

I am continuing to learn and progress by my own up to the point where I have something to say as a developer.

The struggle continues, and so does life!
